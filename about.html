About Tsedar

A physician-curated range for better everyday health. Built to make early, evidence based intervention more accessible, accountable, and easier to act on.

Preventive care should begin before the damage is visible.

Tsedar was built to make early, evidence based intervention more accessible, accountable, and easier to act on.

We did not start with a platform. We started with a practical entry point.

Why we started?

Preventive care is still an underexplored frontier in most urban emerging markets. We recognised the opportunity in functional food as an accessible entry point to engage people in preventive health.

Why functional food first?

We started with functional food because people individually maintain it - is natural, accessible, and helps foster a more nuanced understanding of preventive health to be built.
